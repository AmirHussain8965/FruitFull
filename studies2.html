HOW TO ENROLL

How to enroll in a study
A simple, supportive process

From your first inquiry to your first visit, our team is with you at every step. Each stage is clear, unhurried, and designed to help you make the right decision for your health with full confidence.

1
Initial inquiry

Call, email, or fill out our online form. Our staff will reach out within 1 to 2 business days to discuss available studies.

2
Pre-screening call

A brief 10 to 15 minute phone call to review your medical history and determine which studies may be a good fit, no commitment required.

3
In-person screening visit

Visit our clinic for a full evaluation, including physical exams and lab work, conducted by our medical team at no cost to you.

4
Informed consent review

Our staff walk you through every detail, what's involved, possible risks, your rights, and what to expect. Take your time and ask anything.

5
Enrollment & first visit

Once enrolled, you begin the study protocol. Our dedicated staff remain your point of contact and are available 24/7 throughout.
