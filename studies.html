THE PATH TO PARTICIPATION

How to enroll in a study

We've streamlined our enrollment into five clear steps designed for your comfort. From the first call to your official start, our team ensures a transparent, supportive transition into your clinical trial.

1
Initial inquiry

Call, email, or fill out our online form to express interest. Our dedicated staff will reach out within 1-2 business days to discuss currently available studies.

2
Pre-screening call

A brief 10-15 minute phone call to review your medical history and determine which studies may be a good fit for you no commitment required.

3
In-person screening visit

You visit our clinic for a full evaluation, including physical exams and lab work, conducted by our medical team. This visit is at no cost to you.

4
Informed consent review

Our dedicated staff walk you through every detail of the study including what's involved, possible risks, your rights, and what to expect. You can take time to decide and ask any questions.

5
Enrollment & first visit

Once enrolled, you'll begin the study protocol. Our dedicated staff remain your point of contact throughout the entire study and are available for questions or concerns.

UNCOMPROMISING PROTECTION

Your safety comes first

Your well-being is our highest priority. Through rigorous ethical oversight and continuous medical support, we maintain a secure, private environment where your health is monitored every step of the way.

IRB oversight

Ethical review board

Every study is reviewed and approved by an Independent Review Board (IRB) before it begins, ensuring your rights and well-being are fully protected.

Access to Support

Around-the-clock support

Our medical team is available to assist you whenever you experience side effects or have any concerns throughout the course of the study.

Your right to withdraw

Participation is voluntary

You may withdraw from a study at any time, for any reason, with no penalty or impact on your other medical care.

Data privacy

Confidential records

Your personal and medical information is kept strictly confidential and never shared without your explicit written consent.

Ongoing monitoring

Continuous safety checks

Your health is monitored throughout the study. If any concern arises, we act immediately including stopping participation if needed.

No cost to you

Study care is free

All study-related visits, exams, lab work, and investigational medication are provided at no charge, and compensation may be available.
