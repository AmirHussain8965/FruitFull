Helping you understand clinical research. We're here to provide clarity on every step of your medical research journey.

For Patients

A clinical trial is a research study that tests new medical treatments, drugs, or devices in real people. At Fruitful Clinical Research, every trial is carefully designed, FDA-regulated, and IRB-approved. You'll be guided through every step we believe bringing innovative therapies to our Las Vegas community and surrounding cities starts with ensuring participants feel fully informed and supported.

Each study has specific eligibility criteria, such as age, health condition, or medical history. Simply reach out to our team or complete a brief online registration form, and we'll guide you through the process with no obligation. There's no pressure or commitment at that stage.

Yes, absolutely. Participating in a clinical trial does not replace your regular medical care. We encourage you to continue seeing your physician and inform them of your participation. Our team can also communicate with your doctor as needed throughout the study.

The duration varies depending on the study. Some trials involve only a few visits over a few weeks, while others may last several months or longer. Before enrolling, our team will clearly explain the timeline, number of visits, and what to expect at each stage.

A placebo is an inactive substance that looks identical to the study drug but contains no active ingredients. Some trials use placebos to measure the true effectiveness of a treatment. Whether a study includes a placebo and your chances of receiving one will always be clearly explained during the informed consent process.

Clinical trials go through multiple phases before reaching participants. By the time a treatment reaches Phase II or III trials where most participants are involved it has already shown promise in earlier stages. Our team will explain the study phase and what is currently known about the treatment.

Yes. Participation is completely voluntary. You may withdraw from a study at any time, for any reason, without penalty or impact on your regular medical care. Your wellbeing always comes first — that is the Fruitful promise.

Safety & Ethics

Yes. All trials at Fruitful are FDA-regulated and reviewed by an independent Institutional Review Board (IRB), which ensures the safety, rights, and wellbeing of participants. Safety monitoring continues throughout the entire study.

Your safety is our top priority. If you experience any side effects, our medical team will assess and treat you promptly. All events are carefully documented and reported according to regulatory standards. You will always be supported.

Before joining any study, you will go through a detailed informed consent process. Our team explains the study purpose, procedures, risks, and your rights in clear, simple language. You'll have as much time as needed to ask questions before making a decision.

Compensation

No. Study-related care, visits, and medications are always provided at no cost. At Fruitful Clinical Research, we believe access to innovative therapies should be free for our community.

Many studies offer compensation for time, travel, and inconvenience. The specific details will be clearly explained during the informed consent process before you agree to participate.

For Sponsors

You can reach out through our contact page or email our team directly. We'll respond promptly with a feasibility questionnaire and schedule a call to discuss your protocol, timelines, and patient needs.

We are currently accepting feasibility requests across multiple therapeutic areas. Contact our team to discuss your protocol, and we will provide an honest assessment of our capabilities and patient population fit.

About Fruitful Clinical Research

Our name reflects our mission. "Fruitful" represents something meaningful, nourishing, and impactful. We believe clinical research should bring real, lasting benefits to the community by delivering innovative therapies that truly make a difference.

We are based in Las Vegas, Nevada, and proudly serve Las Vegas and surrounding cities. Our mission is to ensure patients in southern Nevada and surrounding cities have access to innovative therapies, regardless of their background.
